West Cork Coffee
Identity Design

With a focus on quality and doing things the right way, West Cork Coffee's mission was one I couldn't but resinate with.

Based in Innishannon, Co. Cork, West Cork Coffee has been roasting some of the finest coffee in the land the time had come to step up it's branding game.

Working with Tony, Founder and Head Roaster at West Cork Coffee, I designed a simple, scalable and memorable logo that would be come the centre piece all of West Cork Coffee's branding and packaging.

Capturing the personality of both Tony and the business was essential. The WCC logo would grow to mean more than just a coffee brand, but become a symbol for values that West Cork Coffee holds at it's core.

The identity grew from that logo and my work with West Cork Coffee continues as we explore more exciting and fun ideas to grow the brand.
