Luke Slattery is a product designer and frontend developer focused on UX design from Cork, Ireland.

Luke graduated with a BSc. in Computer Science from University College Cork in 2018. He is currently undertaking an MA. in Design Management at Northumbria University through distance learning and is due to graduate in 2022.

Luke is a product designer at Teamwork, working on Teamwork Projects and contributing to the founding stages of the Teamwork Design System. Previously, he led a product redesign at HireHive and has also held internships at Teamwork and Subwoofr.

A passionate product designer and problem solver, Luke thrives on distilling complex problems into simply designed and beautifully engineered products. Utilising his design and engineering backgrounds, he prides himself on seeing projects from the idea stage through to deployment.

Luke loves to travel and jumps at any opportunity to visit his favourite city in the world, New York. His love of timeless, functional design informs his interest in clothing, vintage cars and watches.
