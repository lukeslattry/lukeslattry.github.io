Luke Slattery

Sr. Product Designer

Luke is a product designer and engineer working remotely from Cork, Ireland. He has been designing products, experiences and interfaces for over 8 years.

A passionate problem solver and explorer, Luke thrives on distilling complex problems into beautifully simple products, interfaces and experiences that serve people in a meaningful way.

Luke graduated with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from University College Cork and studied Cognitive Ergonomics & Psychology at the University of Derby.

Currently working at Toothpic, Luke is leading design on Toothpic Professional and coordinating the creation of the Toothpic Design System.
